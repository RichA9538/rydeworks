# PERC Transportation Program - Flat Rate Sheet

## A) Individual Work Transportation (Flat Round Trip)
Home base: South St. Petersburg, FL (rates are flat and zone-based)

| Zone | Service Area | Flat Round-Trip Fee |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| Zone 1 | Core South/Central St. Pete (South St. Pete, Downtown, Midtown) | $18 |
| Zone 2 | Greater St. Pete / South Pinellas (Gulfport, Pinellas Park, Kenneth City) | $20 |
| Zone 3 | Central Pinellas (Largo, Clearwater, parts of Seminole/Safety Harbor) | $22 |
| Zone 4 | North Pinellas - north of Ulmerton (Dunedin, Palm Harbor, Tarpon Springs, Oldsmar) | $25 |
| Out-of-county | Hillsborough $32; Pasco $36; Hernando/Manatee/Sarasota $40 (as needed) | $32-$40 |

## Zone Distance Reference (from home base)
- Zone 1: 0–6 miles
- Zone 2: 6–12 miles
- Zone 3: 12–18 miles
- Zone 4: North of Ulmerton OR >18 miles within Pinellas
- Ulmerton Rd is the reference latitude (~27.9)

## B) Partner Organization Booking - Van + Driver (Flat)

| Booking Block | Zone 1-2 | Zone 3 | Zone 4 | Out-of-county |
|--------------|----------|--------|--------|---------------|
| Half-day (up to 4 hours) | $320 | $360 | $420 | $480-$560 |
| Full-day (up to 8 hours) | $600 | $680 | $780 | $880-$1,040 |

## Rules
- Rates are flat (no per-mile billing). Zone determined by destination area and overall distance from home base.
- Zone 4 used for destinations north of Ulmerton and other higher-distance trips within Pinellas.
- Out-of-county trips use listed county bands. Additional counties priced case-by-case.
- Partner bookings include vehicle + driver within the block. After-hours/weekend premium may apply.
- Rates may be adjusted based on utilization, scheduling, and funding.
