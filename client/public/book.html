Terms & Conditions

Please read and agree to our service terms before continuing.

1. Service Overview

Rydeworks provides non-emergency transportation services ("Service") to enrolled riders. By enrolling, you agree to these Terms and Conditions in full. These terms govern your use of the Rydeworks platform, including booking, payment, and cancellation.

2. Credit-Based Subscription

Upon enrollment, your payment method will be charged $100.00 to establish a ride credit balance. This balance is used to pay for individual trips based on distance-calculated fares. There is no fixed number of trips per week — your balance covers as many trips as the calculated fares allow until the balance is exhausted.

3. Fare Calculation

Fares are calculated based on the distance of each trip using Rydeworks' published fare zones. Fares may vary by trip length. The estimated fare for each trip will be shown at the time of booking. Actual fare may differ slightly based on route adjustments. The average fare is approximately $20 per round trip.

4. Auto-Replenishment

When your credit balance falls below $20.00, your payment method on file will be automatically charged $100.00 to replenish your balance. You will receive a text message notification each time a charge occurs. By enrolling, you authorize Rydeworks to charge your payment method for these automatic replenishments.

5. Credit Rollover

Unused ride credits do not expire on a weekly basis. Any remaining balance rolls over indefinitely until used or until your subscription is cancelled.

6. Free Ride Code

Upon successful enrollment and initial payment, you will receive a Free Ride Code valid for 30 days from the date of issuance. This code must be provided at the time of each booking to apply your credit balance. After 30 days, a new code will be issued automatically when your balance is replenished.

7. No Cancellation During Initial Balance Period

You may not cancel your subscription until your initial $100.00 credit balance has been fully used. This ensures that the service commitment made at enrollment is honored. After your initial balance is exhausted, you may request cancellation by contacting Rydeworks directly.

8. Cancellation After Initial Period

To cancel your subscription after your initial balance is used, contact Rydeworks via text or phone. Any remaining credit balance at the time of cancellation is non-refundable. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

9. No Refunds

All payments are non-refundable. Ride credits have no cash value and cannot be transferred to another rider or redeemed for cash.

10. Rider Responsibilities

Riders must be ready at the scheduled pickup location at the scheduled pickup time. Drivers will wait up to 5 minutes. Missed pickups are charged at the full fare. Riders are responsible for providing accurate pickup and destination addresses. Rydeworks is not liable for delays caused by traffic, weather, or other conditions beyond our control.

11. Service Area

Service is available within Rydeworks' designated service area. Trips outside the service area may be declined or subject to additional charges. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific locations.

12. Conduct

Riders are expected to treat drivers with respect. Rydeworks reserves the right to suspend or terminate service to any rider who engages in abusive, threatening, or disruptive behavior.

13. Privacy

Your personal information is collected solely for the purpose of providing transportation services and processing payments. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required to process payments (Stripe) or send notifications (Twilio). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

14. Changes to Terms

Rydeworks reserves the right to update these Terms at any time. You will be notified of material changes via text message. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

15. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Pinellas County, Florida.
