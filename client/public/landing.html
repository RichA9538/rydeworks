Features

Everything your team needs.
Nothing you don't.

Rydeworks was designed from the ground up for organizations that run transportation programs — not adapted from enterprise software that costs 10x more.

Smart Dispatch Board

Schedule and manage rides in real time. Assign drivers, track trip status, and see your full day at a glance — all from one screen.

Rider Management

Every rider gets a unique ID. Track trip history, notes, accessibility needs, and contact info. Partner org riders get their org's prefix automatically.

Fleet & Driver Tracking

Manage your vehicles and drivers in one place. Track mileage, assign vehicles to trips, and keep driver records current.

Flexible Billing

Charge by the ride or bill weekly. Accept Square, Stripe, ACH, or cash. Issue free ride codes for community outreach. Works with your existing payment setup.

Grant-Ready Reporting

Export trip logs, rider counts, mileage, and revenue data formatted for grant reporting. Never scramble for numbers at the end of a grant period again.

White-Label Portals

Each partner organization gets their own branded portal with their logo and colors. Your brand stays in the background — their experience stays front and center.
